Research

Advancing knowledge in AI, Machine Learning, and Remote Sensing

01 Overview

Developing intelligent systems for real-world geospatial challenges

My research focuses on creating practical solutions at the intersection of deep learning and remote sensing. From land use classification to satellite image analysis and spatial pattern discovery, I work on developing AI-powered tools that address complex environmental and urban planning challenges.

I collaborate with national and international researchers and have received funding from prestigious agencies like DST-ICPS and ISRO RESPOND for innovative research projects.
